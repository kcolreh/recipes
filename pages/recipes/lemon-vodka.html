Recipe for Lemon vodka

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • Lemons
  • Honey
  • Water(boiling)
  • Vodka

Directions

  1. Squeeze a single lemon into a mdeium sized glass
  2. Pour around 150ml of boiling water over the lemon, stir and let it sit for a few minutes
  3. Add honey and 1 shot of vodka(Can add more Vodka if you want a stronger cocktail>
  4. Add as much crushed ice as humanly possible
  5. Enjoy!
