Recipe for Moscow Mule

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • Lime
  • Vodka
  • Ginger beer
  • Honey(Optional)

Directions

  1. Squeeze a single lime in a medium/big sized cocktail glass
  2. Pour vodka,ginger beer and honey into the same glass(1 shot vodka, 3 shots ginger beer, honey to taste
  3. Mix everything well, taste, adjust and fill as much crushed ice as you can
  4. Enjoy
