Recipe for Long Island iced tea

Ingredients

  • Vodka
  • Rum
  • Gin
  • Tequila
  • Triple sec(orange flavor)
  • Sweet and sour syrup
  • Cola
  • Lemon

Directions

  1. Squeeze half of a lemon in a big glass
  2. Pour Vodka,Rum,Gin,Tequila and triple sec in the same glass(half a shot each)
  3. Pour one shot of sweet and sour syrup
  4. Pour Cola to taste
  5. Fill up the glass with as much crushed iced as possible
  6. Enjoy
