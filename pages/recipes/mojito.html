Recipe for Mojito

Ingredients

  • Lime
  • Sugar
  • Mint Leaves
  • Rum
  • Soda Water

Directions

  1. Squeeze one lime into a medium sized cocktail glass
  2. Crush the mint and add to the same glass
  3. Add sugar,rum,soda water and mix it well
  4. Add as much crushed ice as possible
  5. Enjoy!
